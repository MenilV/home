## System Sizing

### Heat Loss Calculation

For ~200m² house in Sarajevo:
- Insulation: Assuming some existing, will be upgraded
- Temperature difference: -15°C outside, 22°C inside = 37°C delta
- Estimated heat loss: 80-100 W/m²

**Required Capacity:**
- 200m² × 90 W/m² = 18,000 W = 18 kW minimum
- **Recommend: 12-14 kW system** (oversize slightly for cold days)
