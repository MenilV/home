Pear Shadow is a software agency I founded in 2020. We help companies build digital products—from mobile apps to Shopify stores to AI-powered solutions.

## What We Do

- **Mobile Development** - iOS and Android apps using React Native and native technologies
- **E-commerce** - Custom Shopify solutions and headless commerce
- **AI Solutions** - Integrating AI into existing products and building AI-first applications
- **Consulting** - Technical strategy, architecture review, and team augmentation

- **JLO Beauty** - E-commerce platform

## Philosophy

We believe in "venture building"—treating every client project as if it were our own startup. This means:

- Deep product thinking, not just implementation
- Long-term partnerships, not transactional relationships
- Quality over quantity in everything we do
