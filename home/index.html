Tehnicke smjernice elektro razvodne mreze

Electrical distribution guidelines

  • 3-fazni glavni ormar, RCD/FI zastita po svim krugovima
  • Podrazvodni ormari po spratu gdje je moguce
  • Cat6 i KNX bus kabl odvojeno od napojnih vodova
  • Plan: oko 70 uticnica i 20 prekidaca ukupno
  • 3-phase main panel with RCD/FI protection on all circuits
  • Subpanels per floor where feasible
  • Cat6 and KNX bus routed separately from power lines
  • Plan target: about 70 outlets and 20 switches total
